“TURKEY INSURTECH AWARDS 2022!” within the scope of Turkey Insurance Fair and Congress!

Turkey Insurance Fair and Congress (USFK), which was held at Antalya Pine Beach Belek Hotel between 03 - 05 March 2022, besides being the largest B2B platform in the insurance sector, offers participants; It provided the opportunity to participate in conferences, panels and events on new investment areas, new technologies and exemplary applications of the sector, and also provided the opportunity to cooperate with many stakeholders. The “Turkey Insurtech Awards 2022” was organized by InsurTech Hub, which evaluates and rewards all kinds of insurance-supporting technological products and projects, from the use of artificial intelligence in insurance to cyber security solutions. Turkey Insurtech Awards 2022, which has a mission to introduce all kinds of products and projects that continue the digital transformation in the insurance industry, is the first and only insurtech award organization in Turkey. In addition to receiving nearly 100 applications, many insurance companies, agencies, brokers, banks, experts and many technology companies that provide digital transformation in the sector participated in the insurtech solutions. The Jury Committee evaluated all the candidate submissions that passed the pre-selection; Fatih Acer, Prof. Dr. Experienced in the insurance industry such as Selim Yazıcı, Cüneyt Kırlar, Özgür Obalı, İsa Turgut İnci, Zeynep Stefan, İsmail Haznedar, Tuğrul Uzun, Sinem Cantürk, Mehmet Abacı, İlker Arabacı, Selcen Gür, Mahir Çipil, Mevlüt Söylemez, Ahmet Nedim Erdem and Levent Korkut and its leading names. Among the award evaluation topics, there are many technologies such as Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), as well as in areas such as the most Insurtech Bank, the most Insurtech Broker, and the most Insurtech Insurance Company. The products and projects that are entitled to receive awards by making a difference with the most innovative ideas and the most up-to-date technologies in the sector were announced at the Turkey Insurtech Awards 2022 as part of the International Insurance Fair and Congress. The finalists and winners of 9 categories are with you! In the “Most Insurtech Agency” category; Dinkal Insurance, which has a range of services where you can get offers in all branches apart from online products, won the first place, while Eron Insurance and Turanlı Insurance made it to the finals. In the “Most Insurtech Broker” category; Sigortam.net, the first and largest digital insurance platform serving in Turkey, won the first place, and at the same time, Complementary Health and Nart Insurance made it to the finals. In the “Most Insurtech Expert” category; Ekol Ekspertiz, who wants to lead and mediate the domestic and international insurance industry and its stakeholders in realizing the digital transformation revolution, won the first place. At the same time, Gla Ekspertiz and SEDEV made it to the finals. In the category of “The Most Insurtech Bank”; Anadolu Bank Insurance Boat Project, which was developed to eliminate credit risks and possible collateral deficiency scenarios, won the first place. At the same time, Anadolu Bank Insurance Platform Project and Emlak Participation Bank made it to the finals. In the category of “The Most Insurtech Insurance Company”; Fiba Emeklilik, which has the fastest and online transaction potential, qualified for the first place, while Anadolu Sigorta and Bereket Sigorta made it to the finals. In addition to many stakeholders that foster digital transformation in the industry, many technology companies that digitize the industry with their technologies were also awarded. In the “Most Insurtech Start-up” category; Agcurate, which produces affordable and accessible insurance solutions for farmers, predictable and efficient, and innovative insurance solutions for insurance companies with satellite analysis, won the first place. At the same time, Wigorta and Valde Bilişim made it to the finals. In the “Most Insurtech Scale-up” category; Lumnion, also known as an artificial intelligence-based pricing platform, won the first place, while Cosure and Tazi AI made it to the finals. In the “Most Insurtech Innovative Product and Project” category; Insurgateway, the first and only open insurance platform that accesses and centralizes the web services of all insurance companies' products and services through a single system, won the first place. At the same time, Turkcell and Sigortapro made it to the finals. In the category of “The Most Insurtech Web & Mobile Application”; Complementary Health, which is located in Turkey's leading online health insurance market, qualified for the first place, while Ortus and OtoKonfor made it to the finals. At the same time, Agcurate initiative, which received the highest score among all winners and was found to be the most interesting and innovative with its technology, crowned its first place with the "Best Insurtech Award".
